WELCOME

CAT or Common Admission Test, the most popular national level management entrance exam ,conducted by Indian Institute of Management for admission to 20 IIMs and more than 100B-schools across India. Around 2.5 lakh MBA aspirants appear for the test every year. CAT 2018 was a 3-hour computer-based test consisting of 100 Multiple Choice Question (MCQ) and Non-MCQ type questions. There were three timed sections, namely Quantitative Ability (QA), Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) and Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR). CAT 2018 was held on November 25 in two slots - Forenoon (9 AM to 12 PM) and Afternoon (2:30 PM to 5:30 PM).

The result of CAT 2018 has been declared on January 5, 2019, and the selection procedure initiates with the release of CAT cutoff. Each of the participating institutes of CAT 2018 will execute their individual admission process, however, the shortlisting would be done based on CAT 2018 cutoff. Next, the shortlisted candidates will be called for further selection rounds, namely, Group Discussion (GD) or Writing Ability Test (WAT) or both and Personal Interview (PI) rounds for the final admission.
